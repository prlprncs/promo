About EMC


In 2019, the College of Information and Communications Technology
at West Visayas State University introduced the course
Bachelor of Science in Entertainment and Multimedia
Computing also known as BSEMC. This course specializes in
Game Development and 2D/3D Animation. It offers a variety
of things such as freehand drawing, programming, sound
designing, game designing, and many more.



What to offer?


ANIMATION


The animation major in the Bachelor of Science in Entertainment
and Multimedia Computing is a rigorous and comprehensive
program that provides students with the knowledge and
skills to create compelling and visually stunning animated
content. Through a combination of theoretical coursework
and practical training, students learn the principles of
animation, storytelling, character design, and production
techniques. Graduates of this program are well-equipped to
pursue careers in animation studios, film and television
production companies, video game design firms, and other
related industries. The program emphasizes creativity,
collaboration, and innovation, and prepares students to be
leaders in the ever-evolving field of animation.

GAME DEVELOPMENT


The game development major in the Bachelor of Science in
Entertainment and Multimedia Computing is an exciting and
dynamic program that prepares students for careers in the
fast-growing field of video game design and development.
This major combines theoretical and practical coursework,
providing students with a strong foundation in game design,
programming, and production. Students will learn how to
develop games for a variety of platforms, including
consoles, computers, and mobile devices, using
industry-standard software and tools. Graduates of this
program are well-equipped to pursue careers as game
designers, programmers, producers, and project managers in
the video game industry. The program emphasizes
collaboration, innovation, and entrepreneurship, and
encourages students to develop their own unique game
concepts and prototypes.
